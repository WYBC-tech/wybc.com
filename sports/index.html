History

The Sports Department has been calling Yale Athletic Events since the very early stages of WYBC. In the 1950s, the Department was even calling events such as Ivy League Swim Meets hosted at the historic Payne-Whitney Gymnasium. Today, the Sports Department is still strong as it calls most Bulldog home basketball, football, and hockey games.
